Install PlanePuck

Flash a fresh PlanePuck right from your browser

⚠️ Use desktop Chrome or Edge — your browser can't access USB serial. ⚠️ Pick the device's serial port when prompted to continue.
  1. Plug your PlanePuck into this computer with a USB-C data cable.
  2. Click Install PlanePuck and choose the device's serial port.
  3. On a brand-new device, accept Erase device; the install takes ~1–2 min.
  4. When it reboots, join the PlanePuck-Setup Wi-Fi from your phone and enter your home Wi-Fi. That's it — everything else sets itself up.

No serial port listed? Install the USB driver for your board and use a cable that carries data (not charge-only).
