Link Spotify

Connect your Spotify account to PlanePuck

  1. Tap Log in with Spotify and approve access — your token appears above.
  2. Copy the token.
  3. On the puck, open the Settings app — that starts the PlanePuck-Setup hotspot and shows 192.168.4.1. (Opening the Spotify app while it's unlinked also offers Open Settings.)
  4. Join PlanePuck-Setup from this device, browse to 192.168.4.1, paste the token under Spotify → Refresh token, and tap Save. The puck restarts and connects.

PlanePuck · the token stays on your device — it is never sent to a server
